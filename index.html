About Me

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer living in Atlanta, GA. I'm originally from New Hampshire and after completing 4 years in the United States Air Force I attended the University of Florida for my undergraduate degree where I majored in Business Management with Minors in Criminology and Asian Studies. Upon graduation I moved to Atlanta, GA and began working for ECI Management. After many years in the apartment industry I decided I was ready for a new challenge and found software development..

Upon realizing I wanted to pursue web development as a career and enrolled in DigitalCrafts' full-time immersive coding bootcamp in January of 2018. Upon completing this program in May 2018 I will pursue a career in this industry.
